Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

We provide comprehensive e-waste management solutions including collection, recycling, and proper disposal of electronic waste.

How do I schedule a pickup?

You can schedule a pickup through our website by clicking the "Start Recycling" button on the home page and filling out the required information of your devices, and confirming your pickup.

What types of e-waste do you accept?

We accept various types of electronic waste including computers, mobile phones, tablets, printers, and other electronic devices.
